Embark on an unforgettable sojourn in the hills with our camping and room stays package at Meesapulimalai. Perfect for large groups seeking both adventure and comfort, this package has something for everyone. Your journey begins with an afternoon pick-up from Munnar, followed by a scenic drive to Meesapulimalai. Experience an unforgettable sojourn in the serene Yellapetty village with our unique camping package. Your journey begins with an afternoon or evening check-in at the rustic charm of Yellapetty. As night falls, indulge in a gastronomic delight with an unlimited dinner, offering both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Wake up with the sun for an exhilarating morning trek, immersing yourself in the raw beauty of the surrounding landscapes. After building up an appetite, return to the campsite for a hearty breakfast before checkout. The package concludes with a comfortable drop-off at the Yellapetty village road, promising to leave you with cherished memories and a yearning to return.
