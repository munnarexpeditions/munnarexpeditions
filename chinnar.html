Eravikulam National Park

Visit Eravikulam National Park which is nestled in the Western Ghats, it boasts rare species like Nilgiri tahr and provides panoramic views of the Anamudi Peak. If fortunate, visitors may witness the vibrant blooms of Nelakurinji. With diverse flora and stunning landscapes, Eravikulam National Park is a must-visit for nature enthusiasts.

Aanaimudi

Anaimudi is the highest peak in South India, and the viewpoint offers panoramic vistas of the surrounding valleys and mountains. Take in the majestic scenery and capture memorable photographs.

Lakkom Waterfalls

Lakkom Waterfalls offers visitors the opportunity to immerse themselves in its crystal-clear waters for a refreshing bath. With its natural beauty and tranquil ambiance, this destination provides the perfect setting for relaxation and rejuvenation. A visit to Lakkom Waterfalls guarantees an unforgettable experience amidst the wonders of nature
