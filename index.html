Chinnar Direction

Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary is nestled in the Western Ghats and is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna. The sanctuary is particularly known for its population of vulnerable and endangered species, including the Indian star tortoise, grizzled giant squirrel, and the endangered Nilgiri tahr. If you're lucky, you might spot some of these remarkable creatures during your visit.

Thekady Direction

Lockhart is a renowned destination known for its tea-related attractions. One of the highlights is the Lockhart Tea Factory, where visitors can embark on a guided tour to learn about the fascinating process of tea production. From witnessing the plucking of tea leaves to witnessing the drying and processing stages, visitors get a firsthand experience of the tea-making journey. Lockhart is also home to Lockhart Viewpoint, offering visitors stunning views of the picturesque tea plantations and the surrounding scenic landscapes. It's a perfect spot to capture memorable photographs and appreciate the beauty of nature. Lockhart is a must-visit for tea enthusiasts, nature lovers, and those seeking a unique cultural experience in a serene environment.
